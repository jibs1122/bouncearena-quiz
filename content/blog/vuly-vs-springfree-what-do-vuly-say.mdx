## What does Vuly consider to be the key differences between them and Springfree?

Vuly's response was candid about their limited knowledge of their competitor: "Don't know much about spring-free other than what customers have told me." Based on this customer feedback, they highlighted several points of comparison:

- **Material concerns**: "Fibreglass springs can easily start to have UV damage and caused fibreglass splinters"
- **Safety considerations**: "Bigger holes in spring-free nets which can be a safety hazard"
- **Durability questions**: They mentioned "a video on YouTube of a carrot snapping in a Springfree trampoline which has since been deleted"

It's important to note that this information represents Vuly's perspective on a competitor product and is based on what they've heard from customers rather than direct testing or comparison. Some bias should be expected when one company discusses a competitor.

This limited feedback doesn't provide a comprehensive comparison between the brands, but offers some insight into what Vuly perceives as potential differences in materials, safety features, and structural integrity.
