## Vuly

Vuly, established in Brisbane in 2007, is a leading name in the Australian trampoline market, known for its innovative designs and commitment to safety.

One of their standout products is the Thunder trampoline, which replaces traditional metal springs with unique leaf springs, offering a safer and more responsive bounce.

Vuly trampolines are designed with no hard edges, and their net poles curve away from the jumping area to minimise injury risks. The materials used are UV-resistant and durable, ensuring the trampolines withstand Australia's harsh weather conditions.

Their range includes models like the Ultra, Lift 2, and Thunder Pro, catering to various needs and budgets, making them a favourite among Australian families for quality outdoor play equipment​.

## Oz Trampolines

Oz Trampolines provides a range of products, including round, rectangular, and in-ground trampolines, catering to various backyard sizes and preferences.

Known for their excellent customer service, they offer easy-to-replace parts and robust after-sales support, making them a trusted choice for families across Australia. Their commitment to safety, quality, and customer satisfaction sets them apart in the trampoline market.

## Jump Star Trampolines

Jump Star Trampolines is a family-owned business based in Perth, Australia. They focus on providing high-quality trampolines and accessories at affordable prices. Their trampolines are designed to withstand the harsh Australian climate, featuring UV-resistant fabrics and high-grade galvanised steel frames.

Jump Star offers a wide range of trampolines, including round, rectangular, and in-ground models, catering to different yard sizes and jumping needs. They also provide various accessories and replacement parts to extend the life of their products.

 With a strong emphasis on customer service and quick delivery times, Jump Star Trampolines ensures a satisfying shopping experience for families across Australia​.

[www.jumpstartrampolines.com.au](https://www.jumpstartrampolines.com.au)

## Premier Trampolines

Premier Trampolines, established in 2008, has built a strong reputation for providing high-quality trampolines designed specifically for the Australian climate.

This Melbourne-based company prides itself on offering durable and safe trampolines that comply with all Australian Safety Standards. Their trampolines feature UV-resistant and mildew-resistant materials, heavy-duty steel springs, and galvanised steel frames, ensuring longevity and safety for users.

Premier Trampolines offers a variety of models, including the Jump and Premier ranges. The Premier range boasts a thicker frame, thicker pads, and PVC safety netting, with sizes ranging from 8ft to 14ft. This range also comes with a lifetime warranty on the frame and an 18-month warranty on spare parts. The Jump range is slightly more budget-friendly, available in black, and comes in sizes 8ft to 12ft, offering a 12-month frame warranty.

[premiertrampolines.com.au](https://premiertrampolines.com.au)

## Mr Trampoline

Mr Trampoline, Australia’s original handmade trampoline maker, has been committed to quality since 1949. Based in Melbourne, they specialise in custom, high-performance trampolines suitable for both families and elite athletes.

Known for their robust construction, Mr Trampoline offers in-ground, above-ground, and deck model trampolines, all designed to withstand heavy use and the Australian climate.

Their products feature two-string performance beds for superior bounce and durability. Renowned for their longevity, some Mr Trampoline units have been in use for over 50 years, making them a staple in Australian backyards.

## What to Look for When Choosing a Trampoline

Choosing the right trampoline for your family involves considering several important factors to ensure safety, durability, and fun.

Here are some key points to keep in mind:

### Safety Features

Safety should be your top priority when selecting a trampoline. Look for trampolines with:

- **Enclosure Nets:** High-quality, durable nets that prevent jumpers from falling off.
- **Padding:** Thick, UV-resistant padding over the springs and frame to protect against injuries.
- **Springless Design:** Consider springless models that eliminate pinch points and reduce injury risks​.

### Frame and Construction

The frame should be sturdy and made from high-quality materials to ensure longevity and safety.

- **Material:** Look for frames made from galvanised steel to prevent rust and withstand harsh weather.
- **Stability:** Ensure the trampoline has a strong base and is stable.

### Size and Shape

The size and shape of the trampoline should match your available space and your family's needs.

- **Space:** Measure your yard and ensure there is enough clearance around the trampoline (minimum 1.5m on each side).
- **Shapes:** Round trampolines are great for recreational use, while rectangular ones are preferred by gymnasts for their superior bounce​.

### Weight Capacity

Check the maximum weight limit of the trampoline to ensure it can accommodate all potential users safely.

### Warranty and Spare Parts

A good warranty indicates confidence in the product's durability.

- **Warranty:** Look for trampolines with long warranties on both the frame and parts.
- **Spare Parts:** Ensure that replacement parts and accessories are readily available for maintenance and repairs.

### Reviews and Recommendations

Reading reviews and recommendations from other users can provide valuable insights into the trampoline's performance and durability.

By considering these factors, you can choose a trampoline that provides safe, enjoyable, and long-lasting fun for your family.
