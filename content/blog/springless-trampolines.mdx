## Vuly Thunder

The Vuly Thunder is a solid option for those seeking an innovative springless trampoline at a lower price point than the Springfree or Thunder Pro. It shares many of the same safety features and build quality, making it a safe and enjoyable choice for families.

We recommend you check out this guide on Vuly’s blog, which outlines the key differences between their models: [Choosing a Vuly Trampoline – Which Model Suits You?](https://www.vulyplay.com/aff/100/?url=blog/choosing-vuly-trampoline)

### Key Features

- **Springless Design**: Leaf springs provide a smooth and consistent bounce.
- **Safety Net**: 180 cm tall net for secure jumping.
- **Sturdy Frame**: Galvanised steel frame.
- **Weight Capacity**: Supports up to 150 kg.
- **Self-Closing Entry Door**: Secure but does not run the full height of the safety net.

- Great warranty

**Cons**:

## Springfree Trampoline

Springfree trampolines are renowned for their safety and durability. They eliminate traditional metal springs, replacing them with fibreglass rods to create a safer bouncing experience. This design reduces common injury points, making Springfree a popular choice among Australian families.

![](/images/posts/springfree-trampoline-3.png)

[View at Springfree](/go/springfree-medium-round)

### Key Features

- **Springless Design**: Uses fibreglass rods instead of metal springs.
- **Safety Net**: Curved poles and fine mesh netting for enhanced safety.
- **Durable Materials**: Galvanised, powder-coated steel frame.
- **Weight Capacity**: Can support up to 700 kg.

### Pros and Cons

**Pros**:

- Excellent safety features.
- High weight capacity.
- Durable construction.
- High resale value.

**Cons**:

- High initial cost.
- Assembly can be complex.
- Zip on the enclosure can wear out over time.

### Our Score:

- Build Quality & Materials: 9/10
- Safety Features & Standards: 10/10
- Bounce and Performance: 8/10
- Ease of Assembly: 6/10
- Value for Money: 8/10
- Additional Features & Accessories: 7/10
- Customer Service: 7/10

### Conclusion

Springfree trampolines are an excellent investment for families prioritising safety and durability. Despite the higher cost and potential assembly challenges, the overall quality and long-term benefits make it a top choice for Australian families.
