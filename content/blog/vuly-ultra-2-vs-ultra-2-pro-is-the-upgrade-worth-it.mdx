## What are the key differences potential buyers should be aware of?

According to Vuly, the Ultra 2 Pro includes several important upgrades over the standard Ultra 2 model:

- **Higher quality frame design** for enhanced performance
- **Thicker and larger springs** creating a better bounce experience
- **Thicker safety padding** for additional protection
- **More durable frame design** suggesting longer lifespan

These differences focus primarily on improved durability and bounce performance, which may be worth considering for families planning long-term use or with more active jumpers.

The information provided by Vuly suggests that the Pro model represents their top-tier offering with premium components throughout its construction, potentially justifying the higher price point for those seeking the best possible trampoline experience.
