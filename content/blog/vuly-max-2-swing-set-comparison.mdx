Trying to work out which Vuly Max 2.1 bundle is right for your family? This guide breaks down the four main options: the Max 2.1 Large, and the three Cubby models (C1, C2, and C3).

All four are built on the same Max 2.1 platform, so they share the same quality and safety standards. The differences come down to size, whether you want a cubby or soccer goal, and which swings are included.

## What's the Same Across All Models

Before diving into the differences, here's what you get regardless of which bundle you choose:

- Same width and height: All models are 193cm wide and 228.5cm tall
- Same weight rating: 80kg per accessory
- Same safety certification: Independently certified to AS/NZS ISO 8124 standards
- Same warranty: 8 years on the frame, 2 years on swings, 1 year on accessories (extended warranty available)
- Same build quality: Galvanised and powder-coated steel with reinforced welded frame
- Same features: Upgraded shade cover with instant-dry mesh, interchangeable accessories
- Same clearance requirement: Minimum 2 metres around all sides

## The Key Differences

### 1. Size and Number of Bays

| Model | Bays | Length |
| --- | --- | --- |
| C1 with Cubby | 1 | 374cm |
| Large | 3 | 484cm |
| C2 with Cubby | 2 | 484cm |
| C3 with Cubby | 3 | 594cm |

You'll notice the Large and C2 share the same length (484cm) despite having different bay counts. This is because the cubby section on the C models adds extra length to the frame.

### 2. Cubby vs Soccer Goal

**Max 2.1 Large** features a built-in soccer goal. This suits families who want a sports-focused setup alongside their swings.

**C1, C2, and C3** all include a built-in cubby with an updated tent wall. The cubby creates a hideout space underneath the frame for imaginative play. You can also add an optional rebounder net if you want the ball-sports element.

### 3. What's Included in Each Bundle

- **Large:** Frame, shade cover, Seat Swing, Spin Swing, Monkey Swing
- **C1 with Cubby:** Frame, shade cover, Large Nest Swing
- **C2 with Cubby:** Frame, shade cover, Spin Swing, Baby Swing
- **C3 with Cubby:** Frame, shade cover, Spin Swing, Nest Swing, Monkey Swing

## Which One Should You Choose?

### Choose the Large if:

- You want a soccer goal built into the frame
- You don't need the cubby hideout feature
- You want three different swing types out of the box

### Choose the C1 if:

- Your yard space is limited
- You have younger children
- You want the cubby but prefer a simpler swing setup
- You plan to add more swings later

### Choose the C2 if:

- You have a toddler and older children
- You want the cubby feature
- You have the same space as the Large but prefer the cubby over the soccer goal

### Choose the C3 if:

- You have a larger yard (nearly 6 metres length plus clearance)
- You have multiple children across different ages
- You want the most complete setup with cubby included

- **Climbing and fitness:** Roman Rings, Gym Rope, Jungle Rope
- **Sports and play:** Max 2 Basketball Set, Rebound Net with Ball and Pump
- **Extras:** Surge Speaker, LED Light, Water Mister

## Final Thoughts

The right choice depends on your yard size, your children's ages, and whether you prefer the cubby hideout or the built-in soccer goal.

If you're torn between the Large and C2 (since they share the same footprint), it comes down to whether your kids would get more use from a soccer goal or a cubby space.

For families with room to spare, the C3 offers the most complete package with both the cubby and a strong mix of swings included.
