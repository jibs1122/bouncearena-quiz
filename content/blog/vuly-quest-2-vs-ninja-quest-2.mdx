For Australian families looking to transform their backyard into an adventure playground, Vuly's Quest 2 range offers specialised options to meet different needs including the standard Quest 2 and the Ninja Quest 2.

Both provide high-quality outdoor play equipment but they differ in features, design, and specialised elements. This comparison will help you determine which system better suits your family's active lifestyle.

## Overview: Quest 2 vs Ninja Quest 2

**Vuly Quest 2** is Vuly's standard monkey bar system, offering adjustable heights, a shade roof, and compatibility with numerous accessories for customised play.

![](/images/posts/large-quest-2.png)

**Vuly Ninja Quest 2** includes a higher set of monkey bars and an integrated Battle Bar.

![](/images/posts/large-ninja-quest-2.png)

## Key Differences

### Design and Features

**Quest 2:**

- Standard single set of monkey bars (120cm – 195cm adjustable height)
- Includes shade roof for sun protection
- Double galvanisation and powder coating for rust resistance
- Unique joiner technology for stability
- Instant-dry mesh material prevents pooling on swings
- Compatible with dozens of accessories
- Height: 264cm

**Ninja Quest 2:**

- Standard features of the Quest 2
- **Higher set of monkey bars** creating a multi-level play system
- **Integrated Battle Bar**
- Taller overall height (308cm vs 264cm for standard models)
- More vertical climbing and hanging options

**IMPORTANT:** If you’re considering getting a Vuly Quest, we highly recommend checking out their [Monkey Bar Configurator](https://www.vulyplay.com/aff/100/?url=monkey-bars/configurator). It lets you play around with different sizes and add-ons so you can see what will actually fit and work best in your backyard.

### Battle Bar & Higher Design

The main features of the Ninja model are:

- **Two-tier monkey bar system** for different play heights
- Battle Bar providing additional hanging and swinging options
- More vertical climbing space
- Better for older children and teenagers

![](/images/posts/battle-bar.png)

## Which Is Right For Your Family?

**Choose Standard Quest 2 if:**

- You're working with a tighter budget
- You have younger children
- You have height restrictions in your yard

**Choose Ninja Quest 2 if:**

- Your children are older or more active
- Your family enjoys ninja-style activities
- Your children want more climbing options
- You have the space for the taller structure

## Conclusion

Both the Vuly Quest 2 and Ninja Quest 2 offer excellent quality and versatility for backyard play. The standard Quest 2 provides a more conventional playground experience suitable for all ages, while the Ninja Quest 2 offers additional features with its Battle Bar and higher monkey bars.

The Ninja model's Battle Bar and multi-level design make it appealing for families with active children who enjoy climbing and hanging activities, while still including all the basic features of the standard Quest 2 system.
