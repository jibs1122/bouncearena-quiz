Exciting news for Vuly fans! The Aussie trampoline brand has recently unveiled a range of updates to its popular trampoline line, bringing enhanced safety features, improved designs, and exciting new options for families.

Here’s what we know so far:

## Dual-Use Accessories

One of the most significant updates is the ability to use both the Basketball Hoop and Shade Cover simultaneously on new Vuly trampolines. This long-awaited feature allows for versatile play options, keeping kids entertained and protected from the sun at the same time.

However, it's worth noting that this dual-use capability isn't currently available for older models like the Ultra 1. But don't fret! Vuly is actively working on developing an attachment to extend this feature to previous models, showing their commitment to customer satisfaction.

## Enhanced Safety Features

### Flexible Net Poles

Safety remains a top priority for Vuly, and their new flexible net poles are a testament to this. These innovative poles are designed to bend under extreme force without breaking or deforming, significantly reducing the risk of injury during vigorous play.

### Instant Setup Ladder

Setting up your trampoline just got easier. The new ladder clips on instantly without the need for tools, streamlining the assembly process. This user-friendly feature is compatible with most older models, except for the medium Flare trampoline.

## Size and Style Updates

### Jumbo Thunder 2

For families needing more bouncing space, Vuly has introduced a larger size in the Thunder 2 model. This jumbo option provides ample room for multiple jumpers or those who simply prefer a more spacious trampoline experience.

![](/images/posts/vuly-thunder-2-pro-trampoline.jpg)

### Built-in Ladder for Thunder 2

In a clever design move, the Thunder 2 and Thunder 2 Pro models now feature a built-in ladder within the frame. This integrated approach eliminates the need for a separate ladder accessory, creating a sleeker overall look.

## Aesthetic and Durability Improvements

### New Colour Options

Vuly has refreshed their colour palette, introducing "Garden Green" and "Warm Grey" pad options. These modern hues replace the previous yellow and blue choices, offering a more sophisticated look for your backyard.

### Enhanced Rust Protection

Durability gets a boost with powder-coated finishes on the Thunder 2, Ultra 2 Pro, and Thunder 2 Pro models. This upgrade provides superior rust resistance, ensuring your trampoline stays in top condition for years to come.

## Redesigned Trampoline Feet

Addressing concerns about stability on soft ground, Vuly has elongated the trampoline feet. This new design helps prevent sinking into lawns, providing a more stable jumping experience across various terrains.

## Anchor Kits for High-Wind Areas

Vuly has introduced a new anchor kit designed for areas prone to strong winds. This kit is stronger than previous versions, providing improved stability for trampolines in gusty conditions.

Key features:

- Enhanced strength compared to older models
- Recommended for securing trampolines in high-wind areas
- Compatible with older Flare and Ultra models

## New Shade Cover Design

Vuly has updated their shade cover design, moving from a full-coverage model to a new flat design. This change has elicited mixed responses from users:

- The new flat design offers certain benefits, though Vuly has not explicitly detailed these advantages.
- Some users have expressed a preference for the old design, which covered the entire trampoline and was more effective at preventing leaves and debris from entering the jumping area.

It's worth noting that the compatibility of these new shade covers with older trampoline models is currently unclear. Customers with existing Vuly trampolines who are interested in the new shade cover should contact Vuly directly for specific compatibility information.

## Limited-Time Offer

In exciting news for potential buyers, Vuly is currently running a promotion. Customers who order a new trampoline by October 8th will receive a free Basketball Hoop and Shade Cover. However, it's important to note that this offer may not apply to the Flare model, so double-check before making your purchase.

## Looking Ahead

Vuly continues to innovate based on customer feedback. They're currently developing attachments to allow older models to use both the Basketball Hoop and Shade Cover simultaneously. For the latest updates on these developments, keep an eye on Vuly's social media channels.
