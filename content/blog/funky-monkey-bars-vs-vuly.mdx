For Australian families looking to bring playground excitement to their backyard, two brands stand out in the outdoor play equipment market: Funky Monkey Bars and Vuly.

Both offer high-quality monkey bar systems with unique features, but they differ significantly in design philosophy, construction, and functionality. This comparison will help you understand which brand might better suit your family's needs.

## Brand Overview

**Funky Monkey Bars** is a family-owned business based in Perth, Western Australia. They pride themselves on Australian-made products using Australian galvanised steel with Dulux outdoor powder coating. Their signature free-standing, height-adjustable monkey bars don't require concrete installation.

**Vuly** is another Australian company well-known for their trampolines, swing sets, and monkey bar systems. Their Quest 2 range features infinitely adjustable monkey bars with modular designs and unique joiner technology, with Pro models adding elevated hideouts.

## Design and Construction

### Funky Monkey Bars

- Free-standing design with patented "twirly-whirly" bars for stability
- Height adjustable from 1.2m to 2.3m
- Australian galvanized steel with Dulux powder coating
- No need for concrete installation - simply pegs into grass
- 10-year warranty on frames
- Load ratings from 750kg to 2,340kg depending on model

### Vuly Quest 2

- Features unique joiner technology for stability
- Height adjustable from 1.2m to 1.95m
- Double galvanization and powder coating for rust resistance
- Pro models include [X-Brace™ frame system](https://www.vulyplay.com/aff/100/?url=monkey-bars/quest-2-pro/large) for additional stability
- 7-year warranty on frames
- Includes shade roof for sun protection

**IMPORTANT:** If you’re considering getting a Vuly Quest, we highly recommend checking out their [Monkey Bar Configurator](https://www.vulyplay.com/aff/100/?url=monkey-bars/configurator). It lets you play around with different sizes and add-ons so you can see what will actually fit and work best in your backyard.

## Which Brand Is Right For You?

**Choose Funky Monkey Bars if you:**

- Want longer warranty coverage (10 years vs 7 years)
- Need a taller height range (up to 2.3m vs 1.95m)
- Appreciate the Australian-made manufacturing
- Want the unique "twirly-whirly" bars for both play and stability

**Choose Vuly Quest 2 if you:**

- Want built-in features like the elevated hideout (Pro models)
- Prefer integrated shade coverage
- Want specialised ninja training features

## Conclusion

Both Funky Monkey Bars and Vuly Quest 2 offer high-quality outdoor play equipment designed to withstand Australian conditions. [Funky Monkey Bars](https://www.funkymonkeybars.com/collections/monkey-bars) emphasises Australian manufacturing, free-standing design, and a longer warranty, while the [Vuly Quest 2](https://www.vulyplay.com/aff/100/?url=monkey-bars) focuses on innovative features like elevated hideouts and integrated shade.

Your choice will ultimately depend on your specific needs: available space, features most important to your children, budget, and long-term plans for the equipment. Both brands offer modular systems that allow for expansion, ensuring your backyard play equipment can grow with your family.
