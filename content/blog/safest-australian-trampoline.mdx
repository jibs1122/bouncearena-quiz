A recent report from [CHOICE](https://www.choice.com.au/about-us/media/media-releases/2021/march/new-choice-tests-reveal-safest-trampoline-springfree-r79-scores-90), Australia's leading consumer advocacy group, has shed light on trampoline safety.

Out of 10 popular trampoline models tested, only one passed all safety tests. One. That's a statistic that should make every parent sit up and take notice.

But don't worry - we're not here to rain on your backyard fun parade. Instead, we're diving into CHOICE's findings to help you understand what makes a trampoline safe, and how to ensure your kids can keep bouncing - safely.

## Key Findings from CHOICE

CHOICE tested 10 trampoline models against the voluntary Australian standard. Here's what they found:

- Only one model, the Springfree R79, passed all safety tests
- The Jumpflex Flex100 passed main structural tests but had minor safety failures
- Eight other models had major safety failures

## What Makes the Springfree R79 Stand Out?

According to CHOICE testing expert Chris Barnes, the Springfree R79 excels due to:

- Strong, durable frame and enclosure
- Protection from impacts against frame, mat, or enclosure
- No significant entrapment hazards
- Clear and easy-to-follow instructions

## Safety Concerns with Other Models

The report highlights several issues with the other trampolines tested:

- Serious entrapment hazards
- Padding or enclosures failing durability tests
- Structural failures

## Springfree: The Gold Standard in Trampoline Safety

"My advice is to get a Springfree, even if it's second-hand," says our trampoline expert, who has assembled thousands of trampolines across Melbourne.

This recommendation aligns with the CHOICE report's findings, but let's delve deeper into what makes Springfree stand out, based on our [Springfree Trampoline review](/springfree-trampoline-review).

### Build Quality & Materials

Springfree's commitment to durability is evident in its construction:

- Galvanised, powder-coated steel frame
- Extra durable net and mat
- Minimal parts requiring replacement under normal use

Our expert considers the Springfree frame to be the longest-lasting on the market.

### Safety Features & Standards

Building on the CHOICE report's praise, Springfree trampolines meet and exceed the Australian Trampoline Standard AS 4989:2015:

- Innovative springless design using fibreglass rods
- Curved safety net poles
- Superfine mesh for a secure bouncing space

Our expert notes, "no springs to pinch you and a strong and flexible net to keep everyone safe," considering it to surpass industry safety benchmarks.

### Bounce and Performance

Despite the springless design, which addresses many safety concerns highlighted in the CHOICE report, user feedback suggests a high-quality, enjoyable bouncing experience.

With a capacity to support up to 700 kg, Springfree trampolines are versatile for users of all ages and sizes.

### Ease of Assembly

While the CHOICE report praised Springfree's clear and easy-to-follow instructions, our expert adds some nuance:

- Professional assembly is recommended if possible
- Self-assembly is achievable with careful attention to instructions and online videos
- "Stickers aid in aligning the net, mat, and frame correctly, simplifying the process"

### Value for Money

Although Springfree trampolines come with a higher price tag, they offer:

- Superior safety features
- Exceptional durability
- High resale value
- Comprehensive 10-year warranty on frame, springs (fibreglass rods), mat, and safety net

### Additional Features & Accessories

Optional accessories like a shade cover and basketball hoop enhance functionality. Note that a ladder and anchor kit are not included in the basic package and must be purchased separately.

### Customer Service and Warranty

While experiences with Springfree's customer service vary, the product's quality generally outweighs service concerns. The 10-year warranty on all major components is industry-leading, emphasizing the brand's confidence in its product.

## Conclusion: Safety First, Fun Always

The CHOICE report serves as a wake-up call for parents about the importance of trampoline safety. While it's concerning that only one model passed all safety tests, it's reassuring to know that options like Springfree exist.

Springfree trampolines emerge as an excellent choice for families prioritizing safety, durability, and quality. While there are minor concerns such as difficult assembly and potential for zip wear, these do not significantly detract from the product's overall value.
