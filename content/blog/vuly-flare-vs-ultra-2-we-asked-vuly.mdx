## What are the key differences potential buyers should be aware of?

According to Vuly, the Ultra 2 offers several significant upgrades over the Flare model:

- **Higher quality materials** throughout the trampoline
- **Larger springs and dual-layered springs** creating a better bounce experience
- **Thicker safety padding** for enhanced protection
- **Thicker frame** for improved durability and stability

These differences highlight that the Ultra 2 is designed with higher-grade components than the Flare, potentially offering better performance and longevity.
