Buying a trampoline for your family is a big decision. You want something safe, long-lasting, and fun.

Jumpflex makes two types of trampolines: the FLEX and the newer HERO. This guide will help parents understand the differences between these two models so you can pick the best one for your family.

## Why Choosing the Right Trampoline Matters

Trampolines are great for backyard fun. They help kids stay active and enjoy being outside. But not all trampolines are the same.

The right one for you will depend on things like safety features, how long it will last, size options, and value for money.

Both the FLEX and HERO trampolines are good quality, but they're made for different needs. As we look at each model, think about:

- How many people will use it?
- How old are they?
- How much space do you have?
- How long do you want the trampoline to last?

These questions will help you decide if the [Jumpflex FLEX](/jumpflex-flex-review) or the HERO is better for your backyard.

## Size Options: Finding One That Fits

Before we get into the details, let's look at the sizes available:

- FLEX: Comes in 10ft and 12ft sizes
- HERO: More choices with 10ft, 12ft, 14ft, and 15ft sizes

The HERO gives you more options if you have a big yard. But remember, bigger isn't always better. Think about your yard size and how many people will use the trampoline.

## How Long Will It Last?

A trampoline's warranty can tell you how long the manufacturer thinks it will last:

- FLEX: 5-year warranty on the frame
- HERO: 10-year warranty on the frame

The HERO's longer warranty means it might last longer. This might indicate which model is best if you want to use the trampoline for many years.

## Weight Limits: Who Can Jump?

How much weight a trampoline can hold is important for safety:

- FLEX: Can hold 150kg in total (one person up to 100kg)
- HERO: Can hold 250kg in total (one person up to 160kg)

The [Jumpflex HERO trampoline](/jumpflex-hero-vs-vuly-ultra-2) can hold more weight. This is good if you have teenagers or adults who want to jump too.

## How High Can You Bounce?

Both trampolines give a good bounce, thanks to their traditional spring design (in contrast with modern [springless trampolines](/springless-trampolines)). However, there are some differences:

- FLEX: Good bounce with 70 springs (on the 12ft size)
- HERO: Better bounce with 80 springs (on the 12ft size)

The HERO's extra springs might give a smoother, higher bounce. This could be fun for more active jumpers or kids who want to learn tricks.

## How Easy Is It to Set Up?

Setting up your trampoline can take some time:

- FLEX: Takes about 2 hours with two adults
- HERO: Can be set up in as little as 20 minutes with two people

According to Jumpflex, the HERO is much quicker to set up. This is great if you're short on time or don't like long assembly jobs.

![](/images/posts/jumpflex-flex-trampoline-materials.png)

## Keeping Jumpers Safe

Both trampolines have safety features, but they're a bit different:

- FLEX: Has a strong safety net and curved poles for more jumping space
- HERO: Has an even stronger safety net and better-designed poles

While both are safe, the HERO's extra features might give you more peace of mind, especially if you have very active kids.

## How Much Do They Cost?

Price is often a big factor when choosing a trampoline:

- FLEX: More affordable (12ft size costs $699\*)
- HERO: More expensive (12ft size costs $1,199\*)

The HERO costs more, but it has more features, a better warranty, and will probably last longer.

\*Prices valid at time of writing.

## Which One Should You Choose?

Picking between the FLEX and HERO depends on what you need. Here's a quick guide:

**Choose the FLEX if:**

- You want to spend less
- You have younger or smaller kids
- You want a good, basic trampoline

**Choose the HERO if:**

- You want Jumpflex's newest trampoline technology
- You have older kids, teens, or adults who will use it
- You want it to last a long time
- You care about quick setup and eco-friendly products

Remember, the best trampoline is one that fits your family's needs, fits in your yard, and will be safe and fun for years to come.

Whether you pick the Jumpflex FLEX or the HERO, you're getting a good trampoline that will help your family stay active and have fun together.
