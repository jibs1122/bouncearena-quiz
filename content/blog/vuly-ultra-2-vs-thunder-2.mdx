Vuly has recently updated their trampoline line, introducing four new models: the Ultra 2, Ultra 2 Pro, Thunder 2, and Thunder 2 Pro. These updates bring exciting new features and improvements.

Let's dive into a detailed comparison to help you choose the best trampoline for your family.

## What's New Across All Models

All four models share some significant improvements:

### Flexible Net Poles

Vuly has introduced super flexible net poles across all of their [new trampoline models](/vulys-new-trampolines). As they state, "This means they can take extreme force without getting damaged or bent!" This enhancement aims to improve both safety and longevity of the trampoline.

### Fresh Colour Options

Gone are the yellow and blue pads. Vuly has refreshed the colour palette with new Garden Green and Warm Grey options, designed to blend more seamlessly with various backyard settings.

### Dual Accessory Use

Unlike previous models, all new trampolines allow simultaneous use of the basketball hoop and shade cover, offering more versatility in play options.

![](/images/posts/vuly-shade-cover-and-basketball.jpg)

### New ladder

Vuly has introduced a new ladder design that clips on instantly without tools. They describe it as "super strong and rigid," made from a single piece of material for added durability.

### Stronger Anchor Kit

Vuly mentions that their new anchor kits are "5x stronger than our previous version," providing extra security and stability.

## Key Differences Between Models

### Spring Technology

- Ultra 2 and Ultra 2 Pro: Traditional coil springs
- Thunder 2: Leaf spring technology
- [Thunder 2 Pro](/vuly-thunder-2-vs-thunder-2-pro): Full-length leaf spring technology, which Vuly claims provides "the smoothest and safest bounce"

![](/images/posts/vuly-thunder-2-pro.jpg)

### Frame Design

- Ultra 2: W-shaped legs, similar to the original Ultra
- Ultra 2 Pro: Straight legs with additional frame ring, reminiscent of the old Lift 2 model
- Thunder 2 and Thunder 2 Pro: Unique design with internal leaf springs

## Model-Specific Features

### Ultra 2

- Single-layered springs
- Standard rust protection
- Vuly describes it as "the next generation in spring trampolines"

![](/images/posts/vuly-ultra-2-pro.png)

### Ultra 2 Pro

- Dual-layered springs for a more powerful bounce
- Enhanced rust protection with powder coating
- Flippable, double-sided padding (Warm Grey and Forest Green)
- Vuly calls it their "most advanced coil-spring trampoline"

### Thunder 2

- Leaf spring technology for a vertical, controlled bounce
- Vuly says it "takes backyard play into the future with Vuly's unique leaf-spring design crafted from a single piece of solid steel"
- New feet design that Vuly claims is less likely to sink into the ground

### Thunder 2 Pro

- Full-length leaf spring technology for the smoothest bounce
- No underside access for enhanced safety
- Slightly larger frame width (415cm vs 409cm for Large size)
- Vuly describes it as "the world's best family trampoline" with "the smoothest and safest bounce"

## Choosing the Right Model

Your choice will depend on your specific needs, preferences, and budget:

- If you prefer traditional spring trampolines and have a tighter budget, the [Ultra 2](/go/vuly-ultra-2) offers great value.
- For a more durable spring trampoline with enhanced features, the [Ultra 2 Pro](/go/vuly-ultra-2-pro) could be worth the extra investment.
- If you're interested in Vuly's innovative leaf spring technology and want a balance of features and price, the [Thunder 2](/go/vuly-thunder-2) could be ideal.
- For those seeking the top-of-the-line model with the smoothest bounce and premium features, the [Thunder 2 Pro](/go/vuly-thunder-2-pro) might be the ultimate choice.

Consider factors such as available space, desired bounce type, safety features, and long-term durability when making your decision. All four models offer significant improvements over previous versions and are designed to provide safe, enjoyable use for families.

Remember, Vuly offers accessories like shade covers, tent walls, and basketball hoops for all models, allowing you to customize your trampoline experience. They also provide a free shade cover and basketball set with each trampoline, adding extra value to your purchase.

Whichever model you choose, Vuly's commitment to innovation and safety is evident in these new designs, promising hours of bouncy fun for the whole family.
