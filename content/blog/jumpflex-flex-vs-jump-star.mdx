Choosing the right trampoline for your family is an important decision that balances safety, quality, and value.

In this comparison, we'll examine how the JumpFlex Flex and Jump Star trampolines compare to help you determine which is the better fit for your backyard and family needs.

## Size and Dimensions

When comparing the 12ft models of both trampolines, they are relatively similar:

|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Dimension** | **JumpFlex Flex (12FT)** | **Jump Star (12FT)** |
| **Mat Width** | 320cm | Not specified |
| **Frame Width** | 370cm | 365cm |
| **Safety Net Height** | 180cm | 176cm |
| **Trampoline Height** | 270cm | 265cm |
| **Mat Height** | 90cm | 89cm |

Both trampolines have comparable dimensions, with the JumpFlex Flex offering slightly taller measurements overall.

## Weight Capacity

Both the JumpFlex Flex and Jump Star trampolines have identical weight capacities of 150kg, making them suitable for children and teenagers.

This capacity is sufficient for most family uses but may be limited for multiple jumpers or adults.

## Spring Technology

Both trampolines utilise traditional spring systems:

- JumpFlex Flex: 70 springs, each 140mm long
- Jump Star: 72 springs, each 180mm long

The Jump Star's longer springs could potentially deliver a deeper bounce. If bounce quality is important to your family, it would be worth testing both models in person if possible.

![](/images/posts/jumpflex-flex-trampoline.png)

## Safety Features

There are some important differences in safety features that potential buyers should be aware of.

- The JumpFlex Flex offers more safety-oriented design elements:
  - Springs external to the jumping enclosure - ses a "SafeSeal" system where the net clips to the jumping mat all around the trampoline.
  - Curved safety net poles to keep jumpers away from the frame, offering 200% more clearance from poles according to the manufacturer.
- Both use zip-closure systems for entry (we generally prefer self-closing/overlapping entry systems as zips tend to fail over time).

## Frame Construction

Both trampolines feature galvanized steel frames designed to withstand outdoor conditions.

In addition, the JumpFlex has additional UV-resistant black powder coating which may provide superior protection against rust and corrosion in the long term, particularly in coastal areas.

## Warranty Coverage

The warranty terms show a significant difference between the two models:

**JumpFlex Flex:**

- 5 years on frame
- 1 year on springs, mat, safety net and pads

**Jump Star:**

- 3 years on frame
- 1 year on springs, mat, safety net and pads

JumpFlex offers a longer frame warranty, which may indicate greater confidence in the structural durability of their product.

## Accessories and Inclusions

Both trampolines offer optional accessories:

**JumpFlex Flex ($749 RRP at time of writing):**

- Ladder (included)
- Basketball hoop
- Shade cover
- Anchor kit
- Slide

**Jump Star ($820 RRP at time of writing):**

- Ladder
- Basketball hoop
- Shade cover
- Anchor kit

## Making Your Decision

Based on the comparison, here's when each trampoline might be the better choice:

**Choose the JumpFlex Flex if:**

- You value the longer warranty on the frame (5 years vs 3 years)
- You prefer the curved pole safety design and SafeSeal system, with springs external to the jumping enclosure for additional safety
- You live in a harsh environment where the powder-coated frame would offer additional protection

**Choose the Jump Star if:**

- You prefer longer springs for potentially better bounce quality
- You don't mind the standard (non-curved) pole design

## Final Thoughts

Both the JumpFlex Flex and Jump Star are quality trampolines offering good safety features and construction. However, the JumpFlex Flex appears to offer some advanced safety features and a longer warranty.

Your final decision may come down to specific features that matter most to your family's needs, your budget, and the available space in your backyard. Whichever model you choose, both trampolines should provide years of safe bouncing fun for your family.
