Looking for the perfect trampoline for your family? We've examined the best options on the market in 2025, comparing safety features, durability, warranties, and overall value.

From budget-friendly choices to premium models, our guide covers four standout trampolines that meet different needs and price points.

## Vuly Ultra 2: Outstanding Value Trampoline

![](/images/posts/vuly-ultra-2-lifestyle.jpg)

[View at Vuly](/go/vuly-ultra-2)

An updated version of an old favourite, the Vuly Ultra 2 stands out as a good value option. It’s the cheapest model on our list but doesn’t compromise on important safety and durability features. Released in late 2024 as part of Vuly's completely refreshed lineup, this latest generation trampoline comes from an Australian-owned company that designs products specifically for our local conditions.

What makes the Ultra 2 special is its dual-coil spring system that delivers an enjoyable, responsive bounce that both kids and adults can appreciate. The recent update featured new flexible net poles designed to withstand extreme force without bending or damage. Other safety features are impressive for this price point, including a superfine mesh safety net, and a convenient self-closing entry system with full-length access that's easier for younger children to use.

Another welcome update is the new tool-free ladder design that clips on instantly and is made from a single piece of material for added strength and rigidity. Note this ladder is an optional accessory.

The Ultra 2 comes with proper Australian AS4989:2015 safety certification, giving parents extra peace of mind. Its galvanized steel frame has a 5-year warranty, with the same coverage for springs and jump mat, while the safety net and pads have a 1-year warranty. It’s worth noting that we do consider the warranty period the biggest downside for this model, with the frame warranty period being half that of the other trampolines on our list.

Despite this, for families looking to get the most bang for their buck without cutting corners on safety or durability, the Vuly Ultra 2 is hard to beat.

## Jumpflex Hero 12ft: Innovation and Durability

![](/images/posts/jumpflex-hero-lifestyle-copy.jpg)

[View at Jumpflex](/go/jumpflex-hero-12ft)

The Jumpflex Hero 12ft has earned its place on our list thanks to its clever design and impressive durability. By eliminating bolts and welds from the construction, Jumpflex has solved two common trampoline problems at once - assembly headaches and long-term durability concerns. Parents will appreciate how much easier and faster set-up is compared to traditional trampolines.

The dual-ring frame structure with its multi-layer anti-rust coating system is built to withstand Australia's harsh weather conditions year after year. The 10-year warranty on the frame is also excellent, complemented by 5-year coverage for springs and mat, demonstrating confidence in the product's durability.

Safety features are thoughtfully designed, particularly the "SafeSeal" system that creates a secure, gap-free connection between the net and jumping mat around the entire perimeter. Kids can bounce with confidence knowing there are no dangerous gaps for feet or fingers to slip through. The outward-curved poles keep the net away from the frame, creating more space between jumpers and the structure.

While this trampoline hasn’t been tested against the Australian AS4989:2015 safety standards, it does meet ASTM F381-16 and ASTM F2225-15(R2020), which are widely recognized international safety certifications.

For families looking for a balance of quality features and reasonable pricing, the Jumpflex Hero is a great option. It’s also the only model on our list that comes with the option of adding a slide!

## Vuly Thunder 2 Pro: Premium Springless Excellence

![](/images/posts/vuly-thunder-2-pro.jpg)

[View at Vuly](/go/vuly-thunder-2-pro)

The Vuly Thunder 2 Pro represents the pinnacle of Australian trampoline engineering. Launched in late 2024 as Vuly's flagship model, this premium springless trampoline delivers innovative design that justifies its position as one of 2025's most impressive trampolines.

What truly sets the Thunder 2 Pro apart is its revolutionary approach to the bouncing mechanism. Instead of traditional coiled springs, it uses full-length leaf springs made from solid steel positioned beneath the jumping surface - an upgrade from the standard leaf springs in the regular Thunder 2 model.

Safety is a key part of the design, with the Pro featuring flexible net poles, superfine mesh, and of course no springs, creating a safer jumping environment. It’s also the only model we’ve ever looked at to feature no underside access, preventing children from crawling underneath. Its impressive credentials include Australian AS4989:2015 certification as well as European EN71 certifications.

Build quality is exceptional, with a powder-coated galvanized steel frame backed by a substantial 10-year warranty. The leaf springs and jump mat are covered for 5 years.

Another recent upgrade is that all new Vuly trampolines now support dual accessory use - meaning you can simultaneously use both the basketball hoop and shade cover, which wasn't possible with earlier models. A further practical improvement is the redesigned feet that are less likely to sink into soft ground.

For those seeking the very best in springless trampoline design and willing to invest in quality that will last, the Thunder 2 Pro is a top choice.

## Springfree Trampoline: Safety Pioneer

![](/images/posts/springfree-trampoline-3.png)

[View at Springfree](/go/springfree-medium-round)

The Springfree Medium Round Trampoline continues to impress in 2025, maintaining its reputation as a leader in trampoline safety innovation.

What makes the Springfree special is its pioneering approach to springless design. Using flexible composite fiberglass rods instead of metal springs, positioned well beneath the jumping surface, the trampoline eliminates common injury points. The company's claim that their design eliminates 90% of product-related trampoline injuries is backed by consistently positive results in independent safety tests.

The trampoline's three-part safety system is what makes it so impressive: a hidden frame that sits beneath the jumping surface, the "SoftEdge" mat (described as 30 times more shock-absorbent than standard safety pads), and the flexible rod system that eliminates gaps and pinch points.

The option to buy wheels for repositioning is a thoughtful addition, and an accessory not available for the other models on our list. It’s not uncommon we see trampoline owners complaining about how difficult it is to move their trampoline.

But what truly sets Springfree apart is its industry-leading warranty - a full 10 years on all components including the mat, net, and frame, demonstrating exceptional confidence in the product's durability.

For families looking for the ultimate in safety, durability, and peace of mind, Springfree offers compelling advantages that justify its premium positioning.
