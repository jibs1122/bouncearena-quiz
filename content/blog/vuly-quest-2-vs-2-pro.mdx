For Australian families looking to bring playground excitement to their backyard, Vuly's Quest 2 range offers two distinct monkey bar system options: the standard Quest 2 and the upgraded Quest 2 Pro.

Both provide high-quality outdoor play equipment, but they differ in features, design, and price. This comparison will help you decide which system better suits your family's needs.

## Overview: Quest 2 vs Quest 2 Pro

**Vuly Quest 2** is Vuly's standard monkey bar system, offering adjustable heights, a shade roof, and compatibility with numerous accessories.

**Vuly Quest 2 Pro** enhances the standard model with an elevated hideout platform, built-in cargo net access, and Vuly's X-Brace™ frame system for additional stability.

**IMPORTANT:** If you’re considering getting a Vuly Quest, we highly recommend checking out their [Monkey Bar Configurator](https://www.vulyplay.com/aff/100/?url=monkey-bars/configurator). It lets you play around with different sizes and add-ons so you can see what will actually fit and work best in your backyard.

## Key Differences

### Design and Features

**Quest 2:**

- Infinitely adjustable monkey bars (120cm - 195cm)
- Includes shade roof for sun protection
- Double galvanization and powder coating for rust resistance
- Unique joiner technology for stability
- Instant-dry mesh material prevents pooling on swings
- Compatible with dozens of accessories

**Quest 2 Pro:**

- All features of the standard Quest 2 plus:
- Built-in elevated hideout with platform
- Integrated [cargo net access](https://www.vulyplay.com/aff/100/?url=accessories/cargo-net-2) for climbing
- Enhanced X-Brace™ frame system for greater stability
- Increased total height (308cm vs 264cm for standard models)

## Accessories Compatibility

Both Quest 2 and Quest 2 Pro are compatible with Vuly's extensive range of accessories including:

- Various swings (Seat, Monkey, Wrecking, Toddler, Bed, Skateboard, Bounce)
- Nest swings (small and large)
- Ninja training equipment (Long Ninja Grips, Round Ninja Grips)
- Sports equipment (Basketball Set, Netball Ring)

- Large: 365cm (L) × 378cm (W) × 264cm (H)

**Quest 2 Pro:**

- Small Pro: 269cm (L) × 240cm (W) × 308cm (H)
- Medium Pro: 365cm (L) × 248cm (W) × 308cm (H)
- Large Pro: 365cm (L) × 378cm (W) × 308cm (H)

Note that Pro models are 44cm taller due to the elevated hideout platform, requiring additional vertical clearance.

## Which Is Right For Your Family?

**Choose Quest 2 if:**

- You're working with a tighter budget
- You prefer a simpler design
- You have height restrictions in your yard
- You want flexibility to add more accessories over time
- You don't need the elevated hideout feature

**Choose Quest 2 Pro if:**

- You want the unique elevated hideout/cubby feature
- You have active climbers who would enjoy the integrated cargo net
- You prefer maximum stability with the X-Brace™ system
- You want a more comprehensive play system out of the box
- You have the budget for the premium features

## Conclusion

Both the Vuly Quest 2 and Quest 2 Pro offer excellent quality and versatility for backyard play. The standard Quest 2 provides a more budget-friendly option with plenty of customisation possibilities, while the Quest 2 Pro delivers an enhanced experience with its unique elevated hideout, integrated cargo net, and stronger stability system.
